## What is Equity Realization?

It is how much of your raw equity you actually capture by the river given positions, opponents, and line. In PLO, multiway pots compress equities and position has outsized impact.

## Factors That Improve Realization

- Acting last (more information, pot control)
- Nut draws with blockers and clean outs
- Hands that retain playability on many turns/rivers
